See also our About Us page for more information.
 

Where are Rosewill products shipped from?
In the United States, Rosewill products are shipped from warehouses in California, Tennessee, and New Jersey.
 

What kind of warranty is available on my product?
All Rosewill products are shipped with a 1-year warranty on all parts and labor. Please complete the form on the Warranty page to register your product(s) with us and secure the proper service should a malfunction occur.
 

How do I return a Rosewill product?
Please click on the "Contact Us" link in the Footer at Rosewill.com. Complete the form and be sure to select the "Customer Support / Return Product" option in the drop-down menu. Our Customer Service Department will contact you with a Return Merchandise Agreement (RMA) number. Follow the instructions from the representative to return your product. 
 

I received the wrong order; what should I do?
At your earliest convenience, please call our Customer Service line at 1-(800)-575-9885 or email us at techsupport@rosewill.com.
 

How can I track my order?
Scroll to the bottom of the page and, under the "My Account" list, click on "Track My Order." 
 

How can I ask Rosewill a question?
Please click on the "Contact Us" link in the Footer at Rosewill.com. Complete the form and be sure to select the "Other" option in the drop-down menu; use the text area to submit your question(s).
 

Can I buy Rosewill products in stores?
Rosewill Inc. products are sold exclusively online. You will find our stores on great websites like Amazon, Newegg, Wayfair, BestBuy, and more. Please click on our "Where to Buy" link the Footer at Rosewill.com.
