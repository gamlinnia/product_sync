Overview

As a predecessor of the Rosewill THOR V2, the Rosewill NightHawk 117 full-tower case provides outrageous capacity with aesthetic style. It supports 12” x 10.3” E-ATX motherboard, eight hard drives, six 140 mm fans, and 420 mm graphics card. Not cool enough? Sure! You can always swap the top fans with a 280 mm liquid-cooling radiator. Tool-less installation of 5.25” and 3.5” devices, easy-access dust filter, and the top I/O panel provides smooth installation and great user experience.
